Executive Summary

RealSteel S Corporation is revolutionizing structural framing with next-generation automated cold-rolled steel technology. We merge precision engineering, software-driven control, and lean manufacturing to deliver stronger, lighter, and smarter framing systems for the construction and modular industries.

Mission: To automate the future of framing — delivering scalable, high-precision, sustainable steel solutions that redefine modern construction.

  • Growing modular and prefab construction adoption
  • Sustainability incentives driving steel demand
  • AI + automation improving manufacturing precision
  • Skilled labor shortages accelerating need for automation
